Features

Everything you need - in one dashboard

Improve performance, efficiency, and stability of your operation

Overclocking

Optimize your machines by applying overclock, underclock, and undervolt to the GPUs. Included tools like BIOS flasher, autotune, rxboost, and AMD Memory Tweak for easier optimizations.

Profit switch

Set up profit switch for your ASICs and GPUs for automated selection of most profitable coin or algorithm. All popular pools, such as F2Pool, Hiveon, Nanopool, NiceHash, Binance, and others, are supported.

Automated watchdog

Beside internal watchdogs on the OS, you can also set up custom event-based triggers and scripts, and hardware watchdogs that will further improve performance and stability of your machines.

Heatmap view

Visually detect and identify most heated areas of your mining establishment(s) and locate positions of the offline machines faster.

Troubleshooting

Track over 800 errors and important events on your 24h logs to learn what is going on with your mining operation or run diagnostic to fix issues on the go.

Smart alerts

Define automated health checks and receive notifications on important events directly to your e-mail, Telegram, and mobile application (iOS & Android).
